Pomegranate Parfait

Ingredients:

  • 1 pomegranate (seeds removed)
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp rosewater
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Edible gold flakes (optional)

Preparation:

  1. In a bowl, mix Greek yogurt with rosewater until well combined.
  2. Layer yogurt, granola, and pomegranate seeds in a parfait glass.
  3. Drizzle honey on top of each layer.
  4. Repeat layering until glass is filled.
  5. Top with a sprinkle of edible gold flakes for an enchanted touch.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the magical flavors.
