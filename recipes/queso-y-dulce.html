Queso y dulce


Queso y dulce is a traditional Argentinian dish that pairs cheese (usually a mild, fresh cheese like queso fresco or queso de mano) with a sweet accompaniment, often a type of fruit preserve or jam. It's a simple yet delightful combination of savory and sweet flavors.

Ingredients

Cheese

  • Fresh cheese (queso fresco or queso de mano) or a mild cheese of your choice, cut into slices.

Sweet

  • Dulce de Membrillo (Quince Paste). This is a popular choice and can be found in Latin American grocery stores.
  • Fruit Preserves or Jam, such as fig, peach, or pear preserves.

How to make it

  1. Prepare the cheese

    • Slice the cheese.

  2. Add the sweet

    • Cut the quince paste or preserves into slices that are about the same size as the cheese.
    • Add the sweet on top of the cheese.
