Unicorn Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ube (purple yam) puree
  • 1 1/2 cups pancake mix
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Butter for cooking
  • Whipped cream
  • Rainbow sprinkles

Preparation:

  1. In a bowl, mix pancake mix, milk, egg, ube puree, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a small amount of butter.
  3. Pour pancake batter onto the skillet, about 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  4. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  5. Stack pancakes on a plate.
  6. Top with whipped cream and a generous sprinkle of rainbow sprinkles.
  7. Dive into your magical Unicorn Ube Pancakes and enjoy!
