Fairy Dust Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 fresh figs (quartered)
  • 2 cups mixed greens (arugula, spinach, and kale)
  • 1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup candied pecans
  • 1/4 cup raspberries
  • 2 tbsp balsamic glaze

Preparation:

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine mixed greens, figs, raspberries, and candied pecans.
  2. Sprinkle crumbled goat cheese over the salad.
  3. Drizzle balsamic glaze on top.
  4. Toss gently to combine all the flavors.
  5. Serve your Fairy Tale Fig Salad chilled and enjoy the delightful mix!
