Mango Sorbet

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes (peeled and chopped)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Juice of 1 lime

Preparation:

  1. In a small pot, combine water and sugar. Heat until sugar is dissolved to create simple syrup. Let it cool.
  2. Blend mangoes, basil leaves, lime juice, and cooled simple syrup until smooth.
  3. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to manufacturerâ€™s instructions.
  4. Freeze for at least 2 hours or until firm.
  5. Scoop into bowls and garnish with additional basil leaves.
