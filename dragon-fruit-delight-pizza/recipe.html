Dragonfruit Delight Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 dragonfruit (peeled and sliced)
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped pistachios
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Preparation:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough onto a baking sheet, and lightly brush with olive oil.
  3. Spread the ricotta cheese evenly over the dough.
  4. Arrange dragonfruit slices over the ricotta.
  5. Drizzle honey generously over the dragonfruit and ricotta.
  6. Bake in the oven for about 15 minutes or until the crust is golden and crispy.
  7. Sprinkle chopped pistachios and fresh mint leaves on top before serving.
  8. Slice and enjoy your Dragonfruit Delight Pizza!
