A group of delicious snacks made entirely of different types of Treasure Insects.

Ingredients:

    Treasure Insect Jam:

  • Treasure insect nest
  • Water (50 ml)
  • Sugar (if needed)

    Coin Bug Crackers:

  • Coin bugs (as many as needed)
  • Oil (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)

    Pearl Centipide Skewers:

  • Pearl centipedes (2)
  • Salt (to taste)

How to cook:

    Treasure Insect Jam:

  1. Remove the eggs and larvae.
  2. Shatter the tiara (nest) and stew together.
  3. Put them in a jar.

    4. Coin Bug Crackers:

  4. Heat some oil and cook the underside of the coin bugs.
  5. Salt them lightly.
  6. Coat them with oil as you stir them.
  7. It looks nice if you flip them with their backs facing up.

    8. Pearl Centipide Skewers:

  8. Remove the legs from the pearl centipides.
  9. Skewer and grill them.
